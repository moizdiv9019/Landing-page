PathAlign didn't come from a startup office. It came from a student who watched his classmates — and himself — graduate confused, and decided to do something about it.

Mohiz Riyaz Shedbale
Founder · 2nd-year Diploma in CS, Government Polytechnic Athani
I'm currently in my 2nd year of Diploma in Computer Science. While learning and building projects, I kept seeing my peers — and myself — stuck on the same question: "I have skills, but what do I actually do with them?" No one around us had a clear answer. That's what pushed me to build PathAlign. The working prototype has already been tested at exhibitions and validated at national competitions. I'm mentored by Annappa Kyamangol, HOD at GPT Athani, who has guided the project's direction from the start.
